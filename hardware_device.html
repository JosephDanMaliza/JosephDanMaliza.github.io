Features

  • 5G Connectivity: Supports high-speed internet connectivity.
  • 90Hz Display Refresh Rate: Provides smoother scrolling and gaming experiences.
  • Dual SIM Support: Allows using two SIM cards simultaneously.
  • 5000 mAh Battery: Ensures long-lasting battery life for daily use.
  • MediaTek Dimensity 700 Processor: Offers efficient performance and power management.
  • High Refresh Rate: Enhances the visual experience for games and video.
  • Design: Slim and lightweight design for better portability.
  • MIUI 12: Custom user interface providing a unique experience over Android.

Specifications

  • Display: 6.5 inches, IPS LCD with a resolution of 2400 x 1080 pixels (Full HD+).
  • Processor: MediaTek Dimensity 700, an octa-core processor.
  • RAM: Options of 4GB or 6GB of RAM.
  • Storage: Options for 64GB or 128GB internal storage, expandable via microSD.
  • Camera:
  • Rear Camera: Triple camera setup with a 48 MP main sensor, 2 MP macro, and 2 MP depth sensor.
  • Front Camera: 8 MP for selfies.
  • Battery: 5000 mAh with 18W fast charging support.
  • Operating System: MIUI 12 based on Android 11.
  • Weight: Approximately 190 grams.
  • Connectivity Options: Includes Wi-Fi, Bluetooth 5.1, and NFC.
