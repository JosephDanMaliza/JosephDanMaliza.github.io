Loki’s Legacy

Loki’s legacy weaves through time and the multiverse, with his chaotic influence shaping the course of history. From the fall of Asgard to the birth of alternate realities, his actions are nothing short of transformative.

Once a villain, Loki's shift to an anti-hero highlights his complexity, showing vulnerability and growth. His journey challenges fate as he confronts his identity and purpose in a constantly changing universe.

Loki's Backstory

Born a prince of Asgard but destined for chaos, Loki’s journey is one of self-discovery and transformation. After facing the TVA, he begins to forge a new path across time and space, confronting his own identity and purpose. As a god of mischief, his actions have consequences that ripple through the multiverse. As he learns about his true nature, he grapples with his sense of belonging and the role he must play in shaping reality. But he soon realizes that the greatest mystery of all lies within himself—who he truly is, and what it means to be more than just the god of mischief.
