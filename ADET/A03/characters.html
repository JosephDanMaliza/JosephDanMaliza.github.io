Meet the Characters

Explore the Multiverse

Step into the world of Loki and meet the key players of the TVA, the Time Variants, and beings who shape destiny across the timelines.

God of Mischief

Loki faces a new journey of self-discovery, from villain to unlikely hero across infinite realities.

Time Variance Authority

An organization maintaining the Sacred Timeline—but secrets, betrayals, and mysteries lurk beneath its golden facade.

Loki Laufeyson

The God of Mischief, pulled from time after stealing the Tesseract, seeks purpose and redemption across the multiverse.

Mobius M. Mobius

A TVA agent who believes in Loki’s potential for change and becomes his unlikely friend and mentor.

Sylvie Laufeydottir

A variant of Loki, determined to overthrow the TVA and uncover the hidden truths of the timeline.

Hunter B-15

A loyal TVA agent who, over time, questions its motives, sparking a journey of self-discovery and personal growth.

Ravonna Renslayer

A high-ranking TVA judge, loyal to maintaining order but grappling with the TVA's true purpose.

Miss Minutes

The TVA's animated mascot—cheerful, mysterious, and hiding more secrets than her friendly voice suggests.

He Who Remains

The enigmatic creator of the TVA, whose death unleashes the multiverse and its infinite possibilities.
