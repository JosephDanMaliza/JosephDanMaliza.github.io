Loki Episode Guide

Welcome to the TVA and Beyond

Follow Loki's chaotic path as he navigates time, destiny, and identity. From the TVA's hidden truths to the fate of the multiverse, every choice shapes the future.

Master of Time

From fugitive to timeline guardian, Loki’s journey reveals the cost of freedom and the power of purpose.

Faces of Loki

Variants, choices, and infinite possibilities—Loki’s greatest battle is not against others, but against himself.

Season 2

Episode 1: Ouroboros

Loki struggles with being "timeslipped" uncontrollably, seeking help from TVA technician Ouroboros (OB) to fix his unstable existence and save the timeline.

Episode 2: Breaking Brad

Mobius and Loki chase down rogue TVA agent Brad Wolfe, uncovering deeper secrets about the TVA's hidden truths and Sylvie’s new life.

Episode 3: 1893

Loki and Mobius track a variant named Victor Timely to 1893 Chicago, facing new choices that could alter the multiverse forever.

Episode 4: Heart of the TVA

As the TVA faces collapse, Loki and Sylvie confront critical decisions about free will, destiny, and the future of all timelines.

Episode 5:Science/Fiction

Loki journeys through memories and timelines in a desperate attempt to master time-slipping and save the TVA from destruction.

Episode 6: Glorious Purpose

In the ultimate test of sacrifice, Loki faces his destiny and embraces a new, unexpected role in preserving the multiverse.
