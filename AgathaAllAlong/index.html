Agatha Harkness Returns

Agatha Harkness returns in a thrilling new series, leading a coven of witches harboring dark secrets. With deceit, betrayal, and revelations from her mysterious past, Agatha's twisted magic threatens to reshape the Marvel Universe. *Agatha All Along* promises a captivating blend of dark humor, powerful magic, and unexpected twists.

In Agatha All Along, Agatha Harkness is stripped of her power until a mysterious goth Teen frees her from a distorted spell. Intrigued by his plea to join him on the legendary Witches' Road—a treacherous magical trial that grants witches what they've lost—Agatha agrees. Together, they form a desperate coven and embark on a perilous journey down The Road, where dark forces and hidden secrets await at every turn.
